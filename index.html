Main Food Items

🍛 Local & Traditional Favorites

  1. Kacchi Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice slow-cooked with tender mutton, potatoes, and signature aromatic spices.
  2. Chicken Polao: A rich, savory rice dish made with clarified butter (ghee) and tender chicken.
  3. Beef Tehari: Cubes of spiced beef cooked with short-grain chinigura rice and green chilies.
  4. Shingara & Samosa: Savory, crispy deep-fried pastries stuffed with spiced potatoes or lentils, perfect as a snack or appetizer.

🍔 Fast Food & Appetizers

  1. Crispy Fried Chicken: Golden, batter-fried chicken pieces served with a side of creamy coleslaw.
  2. Chicken or Beef Burger: Juicy grilled patties topped with fresh lettuce, tomato, cheese, and a spread of mayo on a toasted sesame bun.
  3. French Fries: Classic, crispy salted potato strips—often upgraded with a sprinkle of peri peri seasoning.
  4. Shawarma: Thinly sliced marinated chicken wrapped tightly in flatbread with crisp vegetables and garlic sauce.

Drinks Items

🧋 Boba, Milk Tea & Iced Coffee

Creamy, texturized beverages that combine sweet dairy or plant milk bases with trendy toppings or rich espresso drops.

  • Brown Sugar Boba Coffee: Dark espresso shot layered over creamy cold milk and chewy tapioca pearls.
  • Traditional Boba Milk Tea: Slow-cooked black tapioca pearls settled beneath a smooth, shaken brown-sugar iced tea base.
  • Flavor-infused Boba Shakes: Thick, colorful milk tea options incorporating taro, matcha, or strawberry purees alongside pearls.

🍋 Refreshing Lemon Drinks

Creamy, texturized beverages that combine sweet dairy or plant milk bases with trendy toppings or rich espresso drops.

  • Classic Lemon Mojito: A sparkling, carbonated cooler built with crushed ice, freshly squeezed lemons, and fresh mint leaves.
  • Layered Lemon Mocktails: Vibrant restaurant mocktails made with dense zesty syrups, clear club soda, and beautiful multi-colored fruit garnishes.
  • Fresh Pressed Lemonade: Traditional all-natural chilled lemonade served in clean glasses with floating citrus slices.
